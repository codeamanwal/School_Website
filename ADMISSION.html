RULES FOR ADMISSION AND WITHDRAWAL

THE ACADEMIC SESSION BEGINS FROM FIRST WEEK OF APRIL EVERY YEAR.

1. Registration form for admission along with brochure will be supplied from the school counter against a stipulated payment by cash.
2. Registration form duly filled in and signed by the parents or guardians should be submitted to the school within the stipulated time. At the time of admission, the original birth certificate from the Municipal Corporation along with its photocopy should be produced. Transfer Certificate from the previous school attended by the candidate should be submitted to the school within one month of the start of the session/admission.
3. Admission fdor nursery( for candidates who have completed the age of two years) to Standard II(for students who have completed 6 years) will be finalized on the basis of performance of the candidates through interaction only. From Standard III onwards writyten test followed by interaction will be conducted for admission.
4. Admission of students in class XI (Science / Commerce / Humanities) is based on successful completion of class X examination and on the eligibility criteria prescribed by the school.
5.For all classes parents of the candidates will also be interviewed.
6. Admission will depend on the availability of seats in each class and the competence of the student in the admission test and interview. Canvassing in the form of any request or reccommendation will not be entertained.
7. Management reserves the right to decide on what condition they will admit or retain pupils in their institution.
8. For withdrawing a student from the school, a month's notice is to be given or week' s fee is to be paid in lieu of notice. No TC will be issued until all dues to the school have been paid in full, and then, only on receipt of a written application from the parent or guardian the TC will be issued. The Transfer certificate will be given against charge of Rs. 300.
